---
title: Lookaround
---

Lookarounds can be used to verify conditions, without matching any text.

> You're only **look**ing, not moving.

-   Lookahead
    -   Positive
    -   Negative
-   Lookbehind
    -   Positive
    -   Negative

## Lookahead

### Positive

<Example regex={/^(?=.*#).*$/g}>
    <li>abc#def</li>
    <li>#def</li>
    <li>abc#</li>
    <li>abcdef</li>
</Example>

### Negative

<Example regex={/^(?!.*#).*$/g}>
    <li>abc#def</li>
    <li>#def</li>
    <li>abc#</li>
    <li>abcdef</li>
</Example>

These are commonly used to prevent phrases from matching.

<Example regex={/foo(?!bar)/g}>
    <li>foobaz</li>
    <li>foobarbaz</li>
    <li>bazfoobar</li>
</Example>

<Example regex={/---(?:(?!---).)*---/g}>
    <li>---foo---</li>
    <li>---fo-o---</li>
    <li>--------</li>
</Example>

## Examples

### Password validation

<Example regex={/^(?=.*\d)(?=.*[a-z])(?=.*[A-Z])(?=.*[a-zA-Z]).{8,}$/}>
    <li>hunter2</li>
    <li>zsofpghedake</li>
    <li>zsofpghedak4e</li>
    <li>zSoFpghEdaK4E</li>
    <li>zSoFpg!hEd!aK4E</li>
</Example>

### Quoted strings

<Example regex={/(['"])(?:(?!\1).)*\1/g}>
    <li>foo "bar" baz</li>
    <li>foo 'bar' baz</li>
    <li>foo 'bat's' baz</li>
    <li>foo "bat's" baz</li>
    <li>foo 'bat"s' baz</li>
</Example>

Without lookaheads, this is the best we can do:

<Example regex={/(['"])[^'"]*\1/g}>
    <li>foo "bar" baz</li>
    <li>foo 'bar' baz</li>
    <li>foo 'bat's' baz</li>
    <li>foo "bat's" baz</li>
    <li>foo 'bat"s' baz</li>
</Example>

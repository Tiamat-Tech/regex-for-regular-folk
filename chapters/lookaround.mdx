title: Lookaround
---

Lookarounds can be used to verify conditions, without matching any text.

> You're just **look**ing, not moving.

- Lookahead
    - Positive
    - Negative
- Lookbehind
    - Positive
    - Negative

## Lookahead

### Positive

<Example regex={/^(?=.*#).*$/g}>
    <String>abc#def</String>
    <String>#def</String>
    <String>abc#</String>
    <String>abcdef</String>
</Example>

### Negative

<Example regex={/^(?!.*#).*$/g}>
    <String>abc#def</String>
    <String>#def</String>
    <String>abc#</String>
    <String>abcdef</String>
</Example>

These are commonly used to prevent phrases from matching.

<Example regex={/foo(?!bar)/g}>
    <String>foobaz</String>
    <String>foobarbaz</String>
    <String>bazfoobar</String>
</Example>

<Example regex={/---(?:(?!---).)*---/g}>
    <String>---foo---</String>
    <String>---fo-o---</String>
    <String>--------</String>
</Example>

## Examples

### Password validation

<Example regex={/^(?=.*\d)(?=.*[a-z])(?=.*[A-Z])(?=.*[a-zA-Z]).{8,}$/}>
    <String>hunter2</String>
    <String>zsofpghedake</String>
    <String>zsofpghedak4e</String>
    <String>zSoFpghEdaK4E</String>
    <String>zSoFpg!hEd!aK4E</String>
</Example>

### Quoted strings

<Example regex={/(['"])(?:(?!\1).)*\1/g}>
    <String>foo "bar" baz</String>
    <String>foo 'bar' baz</String>
    <String>foo 'bat's' baz</String>
    <String>foo "bat's" baz</String>
    <String>foo 'bat"s' baz</String>
</Example>

Without lookaheads, this is the best we can do:

<Example regex={/(['"])[^'"]*\1/g}>
    <String>foo "bar" baz</String>
    <String>foo 'bar' baz</String>
    <String>foo 'bat's' baz</String>
    <String>foo "bat's" baz</String>
    <String>foo 'bat"s' baz</String>
</Example>
